What's the end goal?

The question "What are the best" is a a difficult one. It depends on your metric of the best. Some things you should be assessing best on: How well does the program meet your expectations? Can they back it up? Does it look / sound fun? Are they reputable? Are the engineers that are teaching the class reputable or solid developers?
