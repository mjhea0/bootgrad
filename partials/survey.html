What's this all about?


When bootcamps advertise that 90% of their students are hired within 3 months at $80k what does that really mean?


The goal is to answer that question so those looking to join a bootcamp can make an informed decision.


This starts with data collection, which will then be analyzed and presented in an easy to filter manner. It will be simple for you to say see how different metrics, like technolog, location, duration of the porgram, etc. afffect the hiring process and your overall pay.

Please note: The data will become availble after 100 surveys have been filled out.
